Privacy Policy

We value your trust and prioritize the security of your data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide while using our paddy rice monitoring system.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data to provide accurate insights and improve your experience:

  • Sensor Data: Moisture content, temperature, and humidity readings.
  • User Information: Name, gender, age, address.
  • Batch Details: Batch name, type of rice, date planted, date harvested, and date dried.
  • Sack Details: Number of sacks and initial weight (if provided).
2. How We Use Your Data
  • Deliver personalized insights and notifications.
  • Improve predictions on moisture content, drying time, and yield.
  • Enhance system performance and user experience.
3. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access and review your data.
  • Update or correct data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
